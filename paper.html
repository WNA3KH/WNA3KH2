Call for Papers

Dear the speakers at the workshop,

A special issue of the journal Numerical Algorithms on the theme "Recent Progress on Iterative Methods for Large Systems of Equations" will be edited.

Papers should be submitted no later than June 1. All submissions will undergo the usual reviewing process for the journal. The paper could be on the talk delivered at the conference or on another topic, as long as it is related to the theme of the special issue. The length of the paper should be no more than 20 pages.

The issue is planned to be ready for publication in the beginning of 2017. The guest editors will be Keiichi Morikuni, Yuji Nakatsukasa, Lothar Reichel, Jun-Feng Yin, and Ken Hayami.

If you would be interested in submitting a paper to the special issue, please let us know by February 23rd. If possible, please provide a tentative title.

Instructions on how to submit the paper for the special issue will be given later.

(Added on February 24.) When submitting a paper for the special issue, please choose "ITMETH" in "Article type", and then proceed as usual.

Lothar Reichel (Kent State University), Ken Hayami (National Institute of Informatics)
