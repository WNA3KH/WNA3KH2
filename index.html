MARCH 16-17, 2021, ONLINE

Second Workshop on Numerical Algebra, Algorithms and Analysis

in Honor of Professor Ken Hayami's Retirement

Registration

This workshop is dedicated to Professor Ken Hayami on the occasion of his retirement. He will be retiring in March 2021 as a faculty member at the National Institute of Informatics and The Graduate University of Advanced Studies (SOKENDAI). All who are interested in numerical algebra, analysis, and algorithm are welcome to participate in this workshop.
